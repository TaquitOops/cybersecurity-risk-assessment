## Scoring Legend

### Likelihood (L)
* **3 (Certain):** High probability, likely to occur frequently or is an existing vulnerability.
* **2 (Likely):** Possible to occur under normal operating conditions.
* **1 (Rare):** Unlikely to occur, or occurs once every few years.

### Severity (S)
* **3 (Catastrophic):** Significant financial loss, regulatory non-compliance, or total loss of customer trust.
* **2 (Moderate):** Manageable impact on operations or temporary loss of availability.
* **1 (Low):** Minimal impact on the organization's mission or reputation.

### Total Priority (P)
Formula: $L \times S = P$
